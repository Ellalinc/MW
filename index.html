About Me

Hey there! I'm Ella Bazroon, a passionate software engineer on a mission to shape a brighter world. At 27, I'm not just defying odds; I'm embracing challenges and turning them into opportunities.

My journey, fueled by determination and a touch of rebellion against my legal blindness, is a testament to the power of resilience. I see every obstacle as a chance to innovate, learn, and create positive change.

Beyond coding and problem-solving, my passion lies in making technology accessible to everyone. With a rainbow flag as my backdrop, symbolizing diversity and unity, I invite you to join me in this journey of empowerment and progress.
